OncopretR

OncopretR is a multidisciplinary framework that assists in the process of drug discovery and personalized therapy choice for cancers. OncopretR uses the power of transcriptomics and chemoinformatics in tandem with deep learning to model the association between tumor associated gene expression signature and drug efficacy. Below are the salient features of the OncopretR workflow.

  • OncopretR accepts tumor gene expression (both bulk and single cell RNA-seq) data as input. It predicts drug response at cancer sub-clone resolution, thereby allowing clinicians prescribe combinatorial therapy.

  • OncopretR allows in-silico investigation of efficacy of newly formulated or repurposed cancer drugs
