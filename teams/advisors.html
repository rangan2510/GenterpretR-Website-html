Ujjwal
Maulik

Advisor

Dr. Ujjwal Maulik is a Professor and former Chair of Computer Science and Engineering Department, Jadavpur University, Kolkata, India. He has more than 30 years work experience at well known universities and research organizations in Australia, China, France, Germany, Hungary, Italy, India, Mexico, Poland, Slovenia and USA. He has visited more than 50 countries for invited lectures and meetings. He is the recipient of AvH Fellowship, Germany and Senior Associateship of ICTP, Italy. He is the Fellow of Indian National Academy of Engineers (INAE), India, International Association for Pattern Recognition (IAPR), USA and The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE), USA.
