Research

Operating Systems for Precision Medicine. An organized frame.

In the new era of personalized therapeutic, operating system protocols for precision medicine can channelized and synchronized the area of personalized therapeutic. To create huge data warehouse which can help to track the progressive condition of patients. Intervention of AI (specially Federated AI), Network Model and Machine Learning algorithms helps to propose professional applications for day-to-day utilization.

Precision Oncology. Personalized Therapeutic and Diagnostic.

Molecular profiling becomes crucial due to the heterogeneous nature of tumors. This helps to interpret the involvement of genomic variants in the cause or development of individual cancer. Our comprehensive frame tracks the cell-specific and genome-specific modification which can assist the clinicians/ doctors to come up with an appropriate clinical decision. Due to the advancement in computational genomics, we provide personalized malignancy profiling for the patients where the genome-wide transition can be observed.

Precision Medicine for Viral Infection.Genome Based medicine based on the target.

A surge has been observed in the area of viral infection and related studies, specially after Covid-19 situation. Even in SARS-CoV-2 infection, vital organs are individually affected and rate differs in each patient. Our method gathers the genetic and other molecular information of patients and accelerates the virus type identification for unknown viruses. Genome based profiling also assist to create P4 systems medicine.
