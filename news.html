Molecular testing of COVID-19 testing from Saliva

GenterpretR LLC alongside its commercial partners Central Laboratory Indore and Dr. Lal Path Laboratory is launching a robust, rapid, reliable, and reproducible COVID-19 testing facility. Below are some of the key features of our technology:

  1. Unlike other methods, this novel, easy, safe, cost-effective, and non-invasive diagnostic approach allows testing of COVID-19 from patientsâ€™ self-collected saliva samples.
  2. It avoids the painstaking collection of nasopharyngeal swap samples.
  3. It also allows the detection of COVID-19 variants without compromising on the sensitivity of the test.
  4. Due to its microfluidics architecture, the technology allows 6000* tests per day in a high-throughput manner.
  5. Sample collection does not require Viral Transport Media (VTM) and therefore, enables testing of samples from remote areas.
  6. Due to its multiplexing nature test reports will be available within a day.
  7. Our Integrated Fluidic Circuit (IFC) technology both prepares and performs thousands of reactions in Nanoliter volumes, saving time, money, and reducing pipetting steps by 95%.

What is technology?

GenterpretR LLC set up the BioMark HD (Fluidigm), an automated, high-performance PCR/qPCR system that uses microfluidics technology to process samples at nano liter-scale volumes for gene expression, genotyping, sample identification, copy number variation analysis, and digital PCR. Due to its architecture, it allows the processing of hundreds to thousands of samples within a day. For COVID-19 testing, GenterpretR intends to use Advanta Dx SARS-CoV-2 RT-PCR Assay (Fluidigm) and its successor mutational panel assay that collectively allows qualitative detection of nucleic acid from SARS-CoV-2 in saliva specimens and variants detection.

  2. DCGI-CDSCO licensed for use in India by ICMR approved testing labs
